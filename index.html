The Hydrogen 3D Printer

Our under-development Hydrogen 3D printer is designed to be safely and effectively operated as easily as any other home appliance, allowing you to focus on what matters most.

Created with work and education in mind, Hydrogen allows you to quickly and inexpensively explore new ideas. Let your imagination run wild!

Why Hydrogen?

A Tool – Not a Project

Small enough to fit on a desk, Hydrogen is designed as a tool, not a hobby. Unbox and start printing immediately. That’s it.

Powered by AI

Hydrogen uses advanced artificial intelligence and computer vision techniques to ensure safe and smooth operation.

Model Library

Hydrogen enables creativity and productivity by providing a vast array of certified printable models via our mobile app and website.
